Fry Recipe

Description

A crispy and clear recipe for making a tasty homemade french fries

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • water
  • oil
  • salt
  • you can use black pepper or herbs to elevate the taste

Steps

  1. peel and cut the potatoes and soak them in water for about 20 minutes
  2. heat oil in a pan for a minute
  3. fry the potatoes for 3-5 minutes(be careful when you put the potatoes on the pan
  4. remove them and let it cool on paper towel
  5. add a little salt on it and mix it gently
  6. enjoy your homemade fry
