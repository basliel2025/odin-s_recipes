Fry Recipe

Description

A crispy and clear recipe for making tasty homemade french fries.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper or herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes and soak them in water for about 20 minutes
  2. Heat oil in a pan for a minute
  3. Fry the potatoes for 3-5 minutes (be careful when you put the potatoes in the pan)
  4. Remove them and let cool on paper towel
  5. Add a little salt and mix gently
  6. Enjoy your homemade fries
