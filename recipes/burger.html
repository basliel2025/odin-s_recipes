Burger recipe

Description

A delicous homemade burger with grilled beef, cheese and buns. you can add extra topping of your choice like tomato, pickles, lettuce and some sauces of your choice

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Burger buns(prefered toasted)
  • cheese

Steps

  1. form beef patties
  2. grill the patty for about 5 minutes
  3. after we grilled it add cheese on it for 1-2 minutes
  4. gently put the grilled beef on a container
  5. toast the buns with butter for 1 minute and put it in a plate
  6. put the grilled beef with cheese on it inside the toasted bun
  7. enjoy your homemade grilled cheese burger
