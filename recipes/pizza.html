Pizza Recipe

Description

A delicious homemade Italian pizza with mozzarella topping, olive oil and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Mozzarella
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Prepare a dough
  2. Prepare the sauce mix tomatoes with garlic and olive oil and salt
  3. Shape the dough in a circular base
  4. Gently spread the sauce evenly
  5. Add the mozzarella slices
  6. Bake it in oven for 10 minutes
  7. Put the basil topping after you take out the pizza out of the oven
  8. Slice it evenly and enjoy your meal
